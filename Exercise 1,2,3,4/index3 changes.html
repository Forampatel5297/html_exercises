Universal Studios Hollywood's Nighttime Lights at Hogwarts Castle returning in March

Studios Hollywood announced that the ever-popular The Nighttime Lights at Hogwarts Castle at Los Angeles' Wizading World of Harry Potter will return for the first three weeks of March, and the entire final week of next month. The first night kicks off this Saturday
